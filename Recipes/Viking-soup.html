Viking Soup

Description

Very tasty Viking soup recipe.

Recipe relies on the use of Thor's Hammer and onion to add the traditional Viking taste to the food

Ingredients

    Fish or Meat
    Onion
    Thor's Hammer

Steps

  • Get your Chaos blades and slay a grand animal
  • Prepare your meat or fish for cooking with your Thor's Hammer by flattening it
  • Imbue the power of the storm into the meat or fish for the soup
  • Put onion and fish/meat into pot of water
  • Use your Thor Hammer to instantly cook the soup via a lightning strike
  • Cool down the soup
  • Enjoy
