Viking Apple Porridge

Description

Very tasty Viking apple porridge recipe.

Recipe relies on the use of Gungir's spear and apple to add the traditional Viking taste to the food

Ingredients

    Apple
    Apple Sauce
    Gungir Spear

Steps

  • Get your Gungir Spear and slay a great apple
  • Prepare your apple for cooking by getting the skin off using your Gungir spear
  • Prepare apple sauce
  • Porridge and stuff
  • God of War that porridge
  • Enjoy
