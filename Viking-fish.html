Viking Fish&Meat

Description

Very tasty Viking meat or fish recipe.

Recipe relies on the use of Chaos blades and clay to add the traditional Viking taste to the food

Ingredients

    Fish or Meat
    Butter
    Clay
    Chaos blades

Steps

  • Get your Chaos blades and slay a grand animal
  • Prepare your meat or fish for cooking with your Chaos blades
  • Rub some butter into it
  • Wrap it in clay
  • Put the meat/fish into the oven and cook it in the clay
  • Smash the clay
  • Enjoy
